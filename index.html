About Me

I created my first website in 2011 when I discovered the world of blogging and WordPress. As time I went on I became curious about how websites are created from scratch and I taught myself HTML and CSS. From there I learned the fundamentals of JavaScript and PHP and got to a point where I feel confident in turning any design into a fully functioning website/web app.

In 2023, I launched my web development YouTube channel, Practical Web Dev. Since then, it’s grown into a learning platform used by over 40,000 people around the world.

I’ve worked both as a freelance developer for clients and as an independent creator building my own products. My course, The Complete Web Developer Course, now generates consistent recurring revenue each month — all built from scratch with 95% profit margins.
